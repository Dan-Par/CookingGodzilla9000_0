World's Best Chefs and their Signature Dishes

Undoubtedly there are superb chefs in the world, but these are some of the few chefs that have grasped the title of Master Chef.

Wolfgang Puck

Wolfgang Puck is an Austrian chef known to apply high-quality techniques to cheap ingredients and is currently one of the top richest chefs in the world. He includes many fusions in his menus, including Californian, Asian, and French cuisine.

Gordon Ramsay

This English chef is best known for his rants on how the food is horrible, but he is also known to be the best chef on the planet, having a very sensitive palette and mastering all cooking skills before judging another persons take on it.
