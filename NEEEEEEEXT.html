World's Best Restaurants

There are an innumerable amount of restaurants in the world, but there are many that seem to surpass the group and are known for their extraordinary food, quality of service, and skills presented by the chef.

Spago

This Beverly Hills restaurant is founded from Wolfgang Puck, one of the cooking universe's prized members as a chef and restauranteur. It features a menu made from only locally grown and extremely fresh ingredients earned the restaurant the AAA Four Diamond Award and received two stars in The Michelin Guide-Los Angeles edition. It is a little pricey, so it is recommended to come to this 4.7 star restaurant in special occasions.

Shiro

Shiro is a restaurant in South Pasadena known for its Japanese-French fusion cuisine, famous for it's whole, fried catfish and Chinese ravioli. It is pricey, but it is a small price to pay for high quality food. Its founders include Chef Moto and Chef Hideo Yamashiro. The Zagat Guide said quote, "If God cooked fish, this is how it would taste"
